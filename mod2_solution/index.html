Chicken

Chicken can be prepared in a vast range of ways, including baking, grilling, barbecuing, frying, and boil. Since 20th century, prepared chicken is eaten as fast food. Chicken is sometimes cited as being more healthful than red meat.

Beef

Beef is the third most widely consumed meat in the world, 25% of meat production worldwide,after pork and poultry at 38% and 30% respectively.In numbers, USA, Brazil & China are the world's three largest consumers of beef.

Sushi

Sushi is made with medium-grain white rice, though it can be prepared with brown or short-grain rice. Eat with seafood, such as squid, eel, yellowtail, salmon, tuna or imitation crab meat.
